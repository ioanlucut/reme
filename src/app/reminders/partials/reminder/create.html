# How can I add the reminder really fast?
You just need to use @ for the date and time, as in the examples below:
  • Pay rent today @tomorrow 6pm
  • Get ready for the concert @june 6 14:00
  • Dentist today at 2 pm @next monday 8am
  • Take a 30 minutes break @in 2 hours
  • Renew the car insurance @next month
  • My brother’s wedding in 2 weeks @22nd of next month
See more examples.
# How do I make sure the notification email arrives in my inbox?
In rare cases some email providers may mark the reminder emails as spam. If you don't see a notification email in your inbox, make sure to also check the spam folder, or better, add no-reply@reme.io to your address book or contact list.
# What timezone does Reme.IO use?
Each time you create or update a reminder your timezone is detected automatically based on the current time on your device. This means that Reme.IO can send you the reminder email in accordance with the timezone the reminder was created in.

For example, if your timezone is Europe/Amsterdam (UTC+1:00), and you schedule a reminder for June 22, 4pm, you will receive the reminder email at June 22, 4pm (UTC+1:00). As simple as that.

Reme.IO takes in consideration also daylight saving times, so you can forget about timezones. Reme.IO always adapts to you.
# Is my e-mail address safe?
The email address you enter is used for the sole purpose of receiving the reminder e-mail. That’s it. No newsletters or other spam.
